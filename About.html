We are creating a service which you can book, wherein we can pick up your bag in a 100 mile radius from any airport of your choice, fly it from that airport to any city of your choice, and drop it off in a 100 mile radius. We will add RFID tags to each bag to make sure no bag is lost at any stage. We guarantee that your bag will be safely handled and kept properly.


Our vision for the company is that one day, people will walk into an airport without carrying any luggage. We hope that people will be able to comfortably walk into the airport without waiting in long lines to drop off their luggage, knowing that their luggage is being safely transported to their destination. In the future, we plan to expand the service internationally to ensure the safety and timely delivery of everyone's luggage.
